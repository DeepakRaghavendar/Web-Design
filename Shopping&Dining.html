Gifts and Souvenirs

Drop by any of our gift shops to purchase souvenirs to remember your visit to Singapore Zoo.
You can get a small-sized reusable non-woven bag for S$0.30 or a medium-sized one for S$0.50 when checking out. Your small contribution will help reduce plastic waste and fund our wildlife conservation efforts in the region. From an assortment of plush toys to an array of nature-inspired mementos, discover unique finds to bring home and remind you of your visit with us!
