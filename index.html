Amuay is a virgin hill station located on the Western seaboard of Venezuela. The place is located between River Shannon delimiting the to the east and Atlantic Ocean to the west. Most of the regions has extra ordinary land with all the elements of nature. Dense Forests, hills and its humid tropical climate makes the animals and birds to feel like heaven. The whole place is famous for its rich flora and fauna with picturesque forests, which obviously creates chances for large number of wildlife sanctuaries.Amuay wildlife park is located 467meters above sea level. Extending over 52 square km of area, with different landscapes of plantation region, hill slopes and terrain lands. The main attraction of this wildlife park is, it is situated at the centre of the dense forest. A wide range of animals like blackbuck, bison, squirrels, baboon, wolf and Hyena can be seen. Carnivores animals like tiger, lion, panther and jaguar also lives in the park. Other main attractions are reptile section, aquarium, exotic species like Cassowary and Emu.
