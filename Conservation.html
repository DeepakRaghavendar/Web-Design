Make a difference

Variety of conservation projects to conserve endangered species are going on. All contributions are utilised for maintenance of the projects. An acknowledgement receipt for each donation will be sent.

Initiatives to Conserve

A team that focus on usage of earth-friendly items is actively working to ensure resource conservation. Partnership with conservation projects worldwide makes it possible to find volunteers to help these species in overcoming the challenges of survival. "Save endangered fauna" project encourages people locally and globally to put forward their ideas and efforts. Main aim of some of the current projects is to provide special care for specific species.
