DAILY ACTIVITIES



    Activity 1: SNAKE SHOW

    One of the main tourist attractions in the Amuay WildLife Park is the show which happens daily. You will inevitably be offered the chance to view the snakeshow at popular tourist sites around the world. However, a two-hour show for you is likely a very different experience for the viewers.

      Timing : 10 A.M

      Location : McLounge Valley









    Activity 2: CLOSE ENCOUNTERS


    GIRAFFE GARDEN:

    Visitors can get very close encounter with the tallest mammal in the Amuay WildLife Park. We have designated wheelchair zones that can accommodate up to six and 15 wheelchairs respectively. So, even those with limited mobility will have a chance to catch the shows!

      Timing : 12 P.M

      Location : Martin Garden Area



    DOLPHIN SHOW:

    Show is conducted by the well trained staff, here people get a very close view from the stand.

      Timing : 2 P.M

      Location : Water Auditorium










    Activity 3: GOOD EVENING WEIRDOS

    Unique residents of our Zoo is explained to the visitors by the doctors

      Animals : Sloth, Primate, Galapagos

      Timing : 5 P.M

      Location : Balley Room
